ARDUINO DESK CLOCK

FOR EXPERT CRAFTSMEN AND RELIABILITY

At Gamma Electronics we offer solutions that bring joy of electronics to our customers and introduce them the world of electronics.

The Desk Clock is a DIY soldering kit. You only need to provide some basic electronics tools; Solder iron, solder, screwdriver, some fine grit sandpaper and wire snips. We've pre-programmed the chip, so it's ready to roll once assembled.

Designed with hack-a-bility in mind, the clock is based on the same ATMega microprocessor used in the Arduino products. There are extra digital and analog pins broken-out, an open serial port as well as I2C all easily accessible within the case, as well as an integrated prototyping area on-board.
